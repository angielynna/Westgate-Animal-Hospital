About Us

Dr. Kamal Walia has been in practice since 1980 and has served over 150,000 families, offering a great selection of essential vetrinary services for pet owners like you! From routine care to emergency cases, Dr. Walia is here to keep your fur babies in good health and continued happiness. Our clinic is a member of the American Veteranary Medical Association and the Washington State Veterinary Medical Association.
